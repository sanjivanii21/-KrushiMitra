🌾 About KrushiMitra

KrushiMitra is a smart farming platform developed to assist farmers in making better agricultural decisions. The system provides crop recommendations based on soil type, rainfall, season and temperature. Farmers can also access weather updates, fertilizer guidance, disease information, market prices and farm schedules from a single platform.
